ABOUT US

We are a healthy foods service provider that prepare and deliver delicious meals to your door. The dietary needs of seniors are our speciality area due to the extensive experience we have in preparing food for the elderly and the expert input we receive from qualified dietitians.

Better Meals was established in 1993 by people who have an intimate understanding of the needs from seniors for well-prepared meals and a convenient delivery service. Our menus, which feature a wide variety of dishes to choose from, allow us to provide healthy and delicious meals that satisfy the specific diets of our customers.
